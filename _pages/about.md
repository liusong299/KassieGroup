I am a PhD student in Management at the [Lazaridis School of Business and Economics](https://www.wlu.ca/academics/faculties/lazaridis-school-of-business-and-economics/index.html), Wilfrid Laurier University. I earned my MSc in Management (OB/HRM) from Wilfrid Laurier University, and both my Master's and Undergraduate degrees in Management from Nanjing University.

## Research Interests

I have two active research streams focusing on **creativity / innovation** and **team dynamics** respectively.

In my research on **creativity and innovation in organizations**, I investigate (1) the antecedents of creativity within team co-creation contexts and (2) the factors that promote or impede different stages of the innovation process, such as idea evaluation and promotion.

In my research on **team dynamics**, I examine the underlying mechanisms through which conflict escalates over the course of team projects.

I conduct research based on various longitudinal research designs and methodologies, including cross-lagged panel models (CLPM), latent growth modeling (LGM), and latent change score (LCS) modeling.
