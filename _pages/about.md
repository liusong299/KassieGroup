I am an Assistant Professor of Organizational Behaviour / Human Resource Management at the [Lazaridis School of Business and Economics](https://www.wlu.ca/academics/faculties/lazaridis-school-of-business-and-economics/index.html), Wilfrid Laurier University. I received my PhD in Management from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), and my MPhil in Management from Nanjing University.

## Research Interests

I have two active research streams focusing on **innovation** and **organizational deviance** respectively.

In my research on **innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurship**, I take a process perspective to investigate how to effectively manage the innovation process across its multiple stages (e.g., idea generation, evaluation, promotion).

In my research on **organizational deviance**, I examine the antecedents and outcomes of employee deviant behaviors such as workplace gossip and social undermining.

I conduct research using a range of designs and methods, including lab and online experiments, longitudinal surveys (cross-lagged panel design, latent growth modeling, latent change score), social relations modeling, and machine learning algorithms.
